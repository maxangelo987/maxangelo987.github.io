Course Code EE 1
Course Description This course introduces the fundamental concepts, circuit laws, theorems and techniques used in electrical circuit analysis and transient analysis, as well as its application. The course covers circuit topologies and DC excitations, transient response, AC response, and polyphase circuits. The use of computer software for circuit simulation and design are emphasized to expose students to computer-based tools.
