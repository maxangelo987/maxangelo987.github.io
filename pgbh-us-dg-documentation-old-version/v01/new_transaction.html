New Transactions


1. Make a New Transaction

  • 1. You can make a new transaction by clicking the button located at the upper-leftmost side of the main form. See the picture below for reference.

  • 2. A new window will prompt after clicking the specified button. Here, you can input the transaction details and save transactions for approval.


2. New Transaction Form Steps and How it Functions


Note: Please fill up all required(*) fields.

Step 1: Selecting a Claim Type


  • You need to click the "Load" button first and a window will pop-up, showing a list of Claim Types.


  • To select a specific claim type, you can either scroll down on the list of claim types or use the search box located at the top of the list by typing the claim type description, click the claim type chosen and click the "Select" button. You can also select a claim type without clicking the "Select" button by double-clicking the chosen claim type.


Note: On other claim types (e.g. BILLS, SALARIES, i.a.), 2 boxes will show-up where you need to select the Date from and Date to of the claim type, dropping down a calendar, respectively.

Step 2: Selecting a Payee

  • You need to click the "Load" button first and a window will pop-up, showing a list of Payees.

Note: You cannot find a payee if you haven't set its corresponding payee type. (e.g. You cannot find BOHECO I ELECTRIC COOPERATIVE, INC. if you have set the payee type to Others or Employee.)

  • To select a payee, double click the "Payee Name" OR click the "Select" button.
Step 3: Setting the Address

  • To set the address, type it on the address box.
Step 4: Setting the Particulars

Note: Some claim types (e.g. BILLS - AD PLACEMENT AND PUBLICATION, i.a.) have default particulars entered where you can edit or add new particulars.)

  • To set the Transaction Particulars, type it on the particulars box.
Step 5: Set Source and Particulars

  • To set the source and particulars of the transaction, you can either add, edit or remove a source and particulars.
Step 5.1: Adding Source and Particulars

  • Note: You cannot add a source if the office selected have no Work and Financial Plans.
  • For adding a source and particulars, click the "Add" button. Upon clicking, a window will pop-up.
  • The default office that is selected to get funds from will be the office you are in. (e.g. If your office is Office of the Governor, the default office selected to get funds from will be OFFICE OF THE GOVERNOR). To select other offices, click the checkbox located just above the list box of offices.
  • To set a WFP Source, click the WFP Source list box. A list of Work and Financial Plans reference code will drop down on the list. Select the WFP type by clicking on the WFP Type list box.
  • To enter a breakdown amount, type the amount on the Breakdown Amount box. You can click "View Balance" to refer the amount that will be entered. Click the "Add WFP" button if Source and Particulars is finalized.
Step 5.2: Editing Source and Particulars

  • For editing Source and Particulars, select the Particulars you want to edit from the table. After selecting, click the "Edit" button, upon clicking the edit button, a prompt will pop-up. Click "Yes".
  • Upon clicking yes, a password verification would then pop-up, prompting you to enter your password. After entering your password, click the "Proceed" button.



  • You will then be redirected back to the Select WFP form. To utilize the Select WFP Form, refer back to Step 5.1.



  • After editing the inputs, click "Update WFP" button.

Step 5.3: Removing a Source and Particular

  • To remove a Source and Particular, click a specific particular from the list and click the "Remove" button. Upon clicking the button, a prompt will pop-up asking a verification to remove the selected particular. Click the "Yes" button.
  • Upon clicking yes, a password verification would then pop-up, prompting you to enter your password. After entering your password, click the "Proceed" button.

Step 6: Verify the Transaction Amount

  • In order to avoid and notice mistakes, see the Transaction Amount just below the Source and Particulars table. The amount that should show will be the total amount from the added Source and Particulars.
  • If mistakes were committed, you can go back to the earlier steps to correct the mistakes
Step 7: Adding a Responsibility Center

  • To add a Responsibility Center, click the "Respo" button located at the lowermost, center of the form.
  • After clicking the respo button, a form will pop-up where you can select the respo. There are two (2) respo types, Single Respo and Multiple Respo. The default chosen respo type is Single Respo.
Step 7.1: Choosing Single Respo Type

  • After selecting Single Respo type, you can scroll down from the list to find the Respo Center / Fund. You can also use the search box just above the list of respo centers. After finding the specific respo center / fund, select the respo center / fund by clicking the table row in the list.
  • After clicking the respo button, a form will pop-up where you can select the respo. There are two (2) respo types, Single Respo and Multiple Respo. The default chosen respo type is Single Respo.If Single Respo was chosen as the respo type, you cannot edit the breakdown amount nor use the remaining balance of the selected Respo Center / Funds.


  • Note: After clicking the "Finalize" button, you can remove the selected Respo by right clicking the Respo you have added to the Selected Respo list and click remove.
  • After selecting Single Respo type, you can scroll down from the list to find the Respo Center / Fund. You can also use the search box just above the list of respo centers. After finding the specific respo center / fund, select the respo center / fund by clicking the table row in the list.
  • If Single Respo was chosen as the respo type, you cannot edit the breakdown amount nor use the remaining balance of the selected Respo Center / Funds.
  • After selecting the specific Respo center/funds, click the "Add Selected Respo" button. Review everything by the Selected Respo list to verify and click the "Finalize" button.
Step 7.2: Choosing Multiple Respo Type

Note: After clicking the "Finalize" button, you can remove the selected Respo by right clicking the Respo you have added to the Selected Respo list and click Remove.

  • After selecting Multiple Respo type, you can scroll down from the list to find the Respo Center / Fund. You can also use the search box just above the list of respo centers. After finding the specific respo center / fund, select the respo center / fund by clicking the table row in the list.
  • If Multiple Respo was chosen as the respo type, you can edit the breakdown amount or use the remaining balance of the selected respo center / funds.
Step 8: Adding Checklist(s)

Note: Checklist is empty if there were no Claim Type selected.

  • To add checklist(s), click the checklist/s from the checklist list located at the upper-rightmost side of the form.
Step 9: Finalizing and Saving the Transaction

  • You can Save, Finalize and print the document by clicking the "Save and Finalized" button after verifying everything.
Step 9.1: Save for Later

Note: You cannot save the transaction as draft if you haven't entered any credentials on Claim Type, Payee, Address and Particulars.

  • If you have entered credentials but you don't want to save and finalize it for printing and approval, you can click the "Save for Later" button located just beside the "Save and Finalized" button so the transaction will be saved as a draft. You can later find the draft on the Manage Transaction List.
Step 9.2: Refer from Past Transaction

  • You can refer your transaction from past transactions (e.g. drafts, approved, unapproved, i.a.) by clicking the "Refer to Past Transactions" button.
  • After clicking the said button, a form will pop up showing all past transactions in which you can refer to.

  • You can refer a past transaction by clicking the transaction from the list you want to refer to. After selecting the past transaction, click the "Refer to this Transaction" button and you will then be redirected back to the New Transaction form with the credentials from the selected past transaction entered into the redirected form.
